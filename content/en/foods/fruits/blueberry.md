# Blueberry

## Estimated GI

Approximately 53 ± 4 (Low GI, GI ≤ 55)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Rich in anthocyanins — powerful antioxidants that give blueberries their deep blue color
- Good source of vitamin C and manganese
- Higher fiber content compared to many fruits
- Relatively low in sugar

## Serving Suggestions

- Fresh or frozen blueberries have similar nutritional value — enjoy year-round
- Pair with oatmeal or yogurt
- Great in smoothies (avoid adding extra sugar)
