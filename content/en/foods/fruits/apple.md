# Apple

## Estimated GI

Approximately 36 ± 3 (Low GI, GI ≤ 55)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Rich in soluble dietary fiber (pectin), which helps slow blood sugar absorption
- Good source of vitamin C and polyphenol antioxidants
- High water content for satiety

## Serving Suggestions

- **Eat with the skin on** for maximum dietary fiber
- Pectin supports gut health
- Pair with a handful of nuts (e.g., walnuts) to further balance blood sugar
