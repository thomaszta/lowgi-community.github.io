# Spinach

## Estimated GI

Approximately 15 (Very low GI)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Rich in iron, calcium, potassium, and vitamins A, C, and K
- Contains lutein and zeaxanthin for eye health
- Very low in carbohydrates
- Blanching reduces oxalate content for better mineral absorption

## Serving Suggestions

- Blanch before cooking to reduce oxalic acid
- Pairs well with eggs, tofu, and other proteins
- Great for salads, quick stir-fries, or soups
