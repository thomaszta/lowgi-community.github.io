# Broccoli

## Estimated GI

Approximately 15 (Very low GI)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Very low in calories and carbohydrates
- Rich in vitamin C, vitamin K, and folate
- High in dietary fiber
- Contains antioxidant compounds like sulforaphane

## Serving Suggestions

- Steaming or quick stir-frying retains the most nutrients
- Pair with chicken breast or tofu for an ideal low-GI meal
- Frozen broccoli retains most of its nutrients — convenient for any time
