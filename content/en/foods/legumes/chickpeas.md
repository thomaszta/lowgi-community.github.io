# Chickpeas

## Estimated GI Value

Approximately 28 ± 5 (Low GI, GI ≤ 55)

## Nutritional Profile

- Rich in plant protein (~19g per 100g), an excellent protein source for vegetarians
- High in soluble dietary fiber, helps stabilize blood sugar
- Good source of iron, folate, magnesium, and B vitamins
- Low in saturated fat

## Serving Suggestions

- Rinse canned chickpeas before use to remove excess sodium
- Great in salads, hummus, curries, or roasted as a snack
- Pair with whole grains (like quinoa) for a complete protein profile
