# Brown Rice

## Estimated GI

Approximately 66 ± 5 (Medium GI, GI 56-69)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Retains bran and germ, providing more dietary fiber than white rice
- Rich in magnesium, phosphorus, and B vitamins
- Contains natural antioxidant compounds

## Serving Suggestions

- Its GI is still in the medium range — control your serving size
- Cook with legumes (red beans, green beans) to further lower the overall glycemic response
- Soak for 30+ minutes before cooking to reduce cooking time and improve texture
