# Rolled Oats

## Estimated GI

Approximately 55 ± 5 (Low GI, GI ≤ 55)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Rich in soluble dietary fiber (beta-glucan), which helps delay post-meal blood sugar spikes
- Provides sustained, stable energy
- Good source of minerals (magnesium, iron) and B vitamins

## Serving Suggestions

- Choose **whole oat groats or rolled oats**; avoid instant or flavored oats with added sugar
- Pair with protein (milk, eggs) and healthy fats (nuts) to further balance post-meal blood sugar
