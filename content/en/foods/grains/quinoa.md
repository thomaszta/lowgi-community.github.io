# Quinoa

## Estimated GI

Approximately 53 ± 4 (Low GI, GI ≤ 55)

## Nutritional Highlights

- Complete protein with all 9 essential amino acids
- High dietary fiber content, beneficial for blood sugar control
- Rich in iron, magnesium, phosphorus, and B vitamins
- Naturally gluten-free

## Serving Suggestions

- Rinse thoroughly in a fine-mesh sieve before cooking to remove bitter saponins
- Pair with vegetables and quality protein for a salad bowl
- Use as a low-GI substitute for white rice
