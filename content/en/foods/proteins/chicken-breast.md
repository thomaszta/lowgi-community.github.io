# Chicken Breast

## Estimated GI

N/A (Chicken breast contains virtually no carbohydrates — no GI value)

## Nutritional Highlights

- High protein (~31g per 100g), strong satiety
- Low in fat (especially skinless)
- Rich in B vitamins (particularly niacin and B6)
- Nearly zero carbohydrates

## Serving Suggestions

- Marinate with salt and black pepper for 15 minutes before cooking
- Suitable for pan-searing, grilling, or poaching
- Avoid overcooking to prevent dryness
- Serve with plenty of vegetables
