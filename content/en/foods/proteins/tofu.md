# Tofu

## Estimated GI

Approximately 25 ± 5 (Low GI)

## Nutritional Highlights

- High-quality plant protein with all essential amino acids
- Rich in isoflavones — beneficial plant compounds
- Contains calcium and iron (calcium content is higher when made with calcium coagulants)
- Low in carbohydrates

## Serving Suggestions

- Silken tofu for soups and sauces; firm tofu for stir-fries and grilling
- Press firm tofu to remove excess water before cooking
- Absorbs flavors well — versatile for many cooking styles
