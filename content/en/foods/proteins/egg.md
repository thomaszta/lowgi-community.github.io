# Egg

## Estimated GI

N/A (Eggs contain virtually no carbohydrates — no GI value)

## Nutritional Highlights

- High biological value protein with all essential amino acids
- Rich in choline, lutein, and zeaxanthin
- Contains vitamin D, B12, and iron
- One large egg has only ~0.6g of carbohydrates

## Serving Suggestions

- Boiled, poached, or scrambled — all great options
- Pair with vegetables and whole grains for an ideal low-GI breakfast
- Don't skip the yolk — it's nutrient-dense
