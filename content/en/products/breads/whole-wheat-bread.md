# Whole Wheat Bread

## Product Info

- **Brand**: Brand Name
- **Net Weight**: Approx. 400g per loaf (10-12 slices)
- **Shelf Life**: 5-7 days at room temperature

## Estimated GI

Approximately 50-55 (Low GI). The higher the whole wheat flour content, the lower the GI.

## Nutrition Facts (per 100g)

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Energy | ~220 kcal |
| Protein | 8-10g |
| Fat | 3-5g |
| Carbohydrates | 40-45g |
| Dietary Fiber | 6-8g |
| Sodium | 300-400mg |

## Shopping Tips

- Check the ingredient list: **whole wheat flour should be listed first**
- Look for ≥ 6g dietary fiber per 100g
- Beware of "whole wheat" marketing — some products use white flour + bran
- Avoid products with added sugar or high-fructose corn syrup

## Serving Suggestions

- Eat plain or lightly toasted
- Pair with eggs, avocado, or other protein sources
- Recommended serving: 1-2 slices
