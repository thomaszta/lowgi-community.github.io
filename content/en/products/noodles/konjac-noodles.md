# Konjac Noodles

## Product Info

- **Main Ingredients**: Konjac flour, water, food-grade lime
- **Common Forms**: Noodle shape, rice shape, block shape
- **Shelf Life**: 12 months unopened at room temperature (water-packed); refrigerate after opening

## Estimated GI

Approximately 0-5 (Very low GI). The primary component is glucomannan — a soluble dietary fiber that is barely digested by the human body.

## Nutrition Facts (per 100g)

| Item | Amount |

| Energy | ~5-10 kcal |

| Carbohydrates | < 3g (including ~2-3g fiber) |
| Sodium | Varies by brand (choose low-sodium options) |

## Preparation

1. Open the package and drain the liquid
2. Rinse with water 2-3 times to remove the natural odor
3. Blanch in boiling water for 1-2 minutes
4. Drain and add to your soup or stir-fry

## Shopping Tips

- Choose **no added sugar** varieties
- Check sodium content — some products are high in salt
- Water-packed varieties generally have better texture than shelf-stable ones

## Serving Suggestions

- Konjac noodles have little flavor on their own — pair with a flavorful broth or sauce
- Combine with protein (chicken, tofu, eggs) and vegetables for a balanced meal
- Great substitute for rice noodles or pasta in soups and cold dishes
