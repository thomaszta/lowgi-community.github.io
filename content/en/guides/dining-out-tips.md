# Dining Out Tips

## Ordering Strategy

- **Choose grilled, steamed, or poached** dishes; avoid fried or braised
- **Ask for sauces and dressings on the side**
- **Substitute vegetables** for rice or noodles as a side
- Choose clear broth soups instead of creamy soups

## Beverages

- Stick with water, unsweetened tea, or black coffee
- Avoid sugary drinks, juice, and sweetened wine
- If drinking alcohol, choose dry wine and limit the amount

## Staple Alternatives

- Request **less rice** or choose mixed grains when available
- At hot pot, load up on vegetables and lean meat; skip meatballs and rice cakes
- For noodles, choose soup noodles over dry-tossed or fried noodles

## Eating Order

For better blood sugar control, eat in this order:

1. Vegetables first
2. Protein next (meat, eggs, tofu)
3. Staple foods last

This sequence helps slow the post-meal blood sugar rise.
