# Grilled Chicken Salad

## Ingredients

- [Chicken breast](/en/foods/proteins/chicken-breast.md) 150g
- [Broccoli](/en/foods/vegetables/broccoli.md) 100g
- Mixed salad greens 100g
- Cherry tomatoes 5
- [Quinoa](/en/foods/grains/quinoa.md) 30g (pre-cooked)
- Olive oil 2 tsp
- Lemon juice 1 tbsp
- Salt, black pepper, rosemary to taste

## Instructions

1. Marinate chicken breast with salt, pepper, rosemary, and 1 tsp olive oil for 15 minutes
2. Grill or pan-sear chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through
3. Rest chicken for 5 minutes, then slice
4. Blanch broccoli for 1 minute, then rinse under cold water and drain
5. In a large bowl, combine salad greens, broccoli, halved cherry tomatoes, and cooked quinoa
6. Top with sliced chicken, drizzle with remaining olive oil and lemon juice, and toss

## Nutritional Analysis

- GI: Approximately 35 (Low GI)
- Protein: ~35g — highly satiating
- Rich in dietary fiber and multiple vitamins

## Tips

- Marinate chicken for 2+ hours for deeper flavor
- Cook a larger batch of quinoa and refrigerate for quick use throughout the week
