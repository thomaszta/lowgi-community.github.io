# Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

## Ingredients

- Plain Greek yogurt 150g
- [Blueberries](/en/foods/fruits/blueberry/) 30g
- Strawberries 3 (sliced)
- Unsweetened coconut flakes 1 tbsp
- Chia seeds 1 tsp (optional)

## Instructions

1. Take a clear glass or small bowl
2. Layer half of the Greek yogurt at the bottom
3. Add half of the blueberries and strawberry slices
4. Layer the remaining yogurt on top
5. Finish with remaining berries, coconut flakes, and chia seeds

## Nutritional Analysis

- GI: Approximately 30 (Very low GI)
- Protein: ~12g
- Natural sweetness comes entirely from the berries — no added sugar needed

## Tips

- Use **plain unsweetened** Greek yogurt to avoid added sugars
- Chia seeds add extra dietary fiber and omega-3 fatty acids
- Works as a quick breakfast or a healthy snack
