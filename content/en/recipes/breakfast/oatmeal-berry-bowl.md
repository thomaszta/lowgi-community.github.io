# Oatmeal Berry Bowl

## Ingredients

- [Rolled oats](/en/foods/grains/rolled-oats.md) 40g
- [Blueberries](/en/foods/fruits/blueberry.md) 50g
- Milk (or almond milk) 200ml
- Walnuts 15g (chopped)
- Ground cinnamon (optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine rolled oats and milk in a small saucepan. Cook over low heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally
2. Remove from heat once the oats are tender and the mixture is thick
3. Transfer to a bowl and top with blueberries and chopped walnuts
4. Sprinkle with cinnamon if desired

## Nutritional Analysis

- GI: Approximately 50 (Low GI range)
- The protein and fat content help further slow blood sugar response
- Total dietary fiber: ~8g

## Tips

- Overnight oats version: mix oats and milk, refrigerate overnight, then add fruit and nuts in the morning
- Blueberries can be substituted with other berries
