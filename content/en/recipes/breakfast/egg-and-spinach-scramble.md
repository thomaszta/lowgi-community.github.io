# Egg and Spinach Scramble

## Ingredients

- [Eggs](/en/foods/proteins/egg.md) 2
- [Spinach](/en/foods/vegetables/spinach.md) 100g
- Olive oil 1 tsp
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Garlic 1 clove (sliced, optional)

## Instructions

1. Blanch spinach, drain well, and chop
2. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt
3. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic if using
4. Pour in eggs and stir until half-set
5. Add spinach and toss well. Season with black pepper and serve

## Nutritional Analysis

- GI: Very low (main ingredients contain minimal carbohydrates)
- Protein: ~15g — an excellent breakfast choice
- Pairs well with a slice of whole-grain toast for a complete meal

## Tips

- Blanching spinach removes oxalic acid for better flavor and nutrient absorption
- Add a sprinkle of cheese for extra flavor
